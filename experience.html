Experience & Skills

Professional Experience

Netcompany A/S – Consultant to Managing Architect

2019 – Present

I began my career at Netcompany in 2019 as a Consultant in the public sector, working with .NET Framework, ASP.NET MVC, SQL, and SharePoint. Within a few months, I successfully launched my first major solution, exceeding client expectations and leading to a four-year contract extension.

In 2022, I transitioned into the private sector, joining the development of AIRHART, a real-time control tower system under the Smarter Airports initiative. Shortly after, I became team lead in charge of generalizing AIRHART for broader business applications beyond airports. This resulted in Pulse, now widely used across industries and recognized with Best in System Design at the Danish Digital Awards 2024.

In 2024, I advanced to an Architect role, shifting focus to technical leadership. My key contributions included designing AI/ML-powered extensions for Pulse, enhancing data processing through intelligent enrichment.

Now, as Managing Architect (2025 – Present), I serve as the tech lead, overseeing the design and architecture of major software solutions centered around Pulse. My current responsibility is implementing Pulse for one of Europe’s busiest airports, ensuring seamless integration and operational efficiency.

AUGIT Solutions – Co-founder & CTO

2018 – 2019

I co-founded a company specializing in augmented reality solutions for trucks, aiming to eliminate blind spots using AR glasses and GoPro cameras. My master's thesis demonstrated its effectiveness in enhancing driver visibility.

XMedicus ApS – Student Programmer & Software Consultant

2017 – 2019

My journey at XMedicus began as a student programmer, where I developed Android and iOS apps for medical software systems using Xamarin. This role gave me hands-on experience in mobile healthcare solutions, applying software engineering to real-world medical applications.

After completing my studies in 2019, XMedicus offered me a permanent role as a Software Consultant, working on advanced healthcare technology. Though my tenure was brief before transitioning to Netcompany, I refined my expertise in mobile development and healthcare-focused solutions.
